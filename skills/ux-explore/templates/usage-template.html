Add an RSS feed

Purpose
Subscribe to a new RSS source.
Entry point
"Add feed" button in the sidebar.
Steps
  1. Click "Add feed".
  2. Paste an RSS URL into the feed URL field.
  3. Click "Add".
  4. Confirm the feed appears in the feed list.
Result
The app subscribes to the feed and starts fetching items.
Related controls
Refresh feed, Open item, Remove feed.
